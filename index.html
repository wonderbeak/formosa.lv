Our main goal is to show you the different faces and moods of the Baltics, both past and present.

Formosa is a Latvian tourism agency which offers a wide range of services within the Baltic States (Latvia, Estonia, Lithuania). We offer both experience and innovation and our staff consists of highly-skilled guides and travel planners, whose passion is to make your leisure special and serve a palette of unforgettable experiences.

HOW WE WORK

How do we work?

First of all – we work exceptionally well, thank you very much! On a more serious note, we tend to look into every client’s request and fulfill your every wish, be it a trip through the Baltic historical sights, a visit to abandoned army bunkers deep underground or a culinary journey.

We WILL:

  • Plan the most exciting journey for you in accordance with your wishes and goals
  • Find the best hotels, restaurants and places to visit
  • Amaze you with “our” cities

We WON'T:

OUR GUIDES

If you want to make a reservation for a tour, have any questions about the services we offer or have a co-operation proposal - feel free to use any of the contact details below. Call us, write to us, tell us what you want. It’s as simple as that. In return we will respond in the shortest time and tell you what we CAN do for you and how much that will be.

Alisha

Our most experienced, exciting and extraordinary guide. Alisha has been the main guide for Asian guests for more than a decade. Her special approach to "guiding" is the foundation on which Farmosa is built. She sees guides as people who introduce their old friends (the city) to their new acquaintances (guests). In her opinion, this interaction usually brings out the best in all parties.

Askold

A rebel and revolutionary in the Latvian tourism business - Askold has designed our "underground" tours. He has great knowledge of Baltic history during the Soviet period and is very passionate about alternative tours that are out of the ordinary. He is also in touch with the Baltic youth and knows about all of the latest tendencies in Latvian life.

Vito

He knows the dates and he’s never late. Vito comes from a long line of local tour guides. Having a great knowledge of world history and work experience in Latvia, Austria and the UK, he is keen on putting everything into international perspective. You can be sure to learn a thing or two from this young man. The locals refer to him as the “Marlboro Man”. Book your tour now and find out why!
